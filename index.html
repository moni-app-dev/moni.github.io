About the App

Stay Focused, Achieve More!

In today's fast-paced world, distractions are everywhere. Whether it's social media, games, or endless notifications, it's all too easy to lose focus. That's where Moni comes in!

Key Features:

- App Locking: Keep all other apps inaccessible at the start of your day until you complete a focused task.

- Single-task Focus: Choose one task to work on, ensuring you dedicate your attention to what truly matters.

- User-friendly Interface: Easy to navigate, simple to set up, and seamless to use.

How It Works

At the start of your day, activate Focus Lock. All other apps will be temporarily blocked until you complete your designated task. This innovative approach helps you cultivate discipline and maintain your focus throughout the day.
